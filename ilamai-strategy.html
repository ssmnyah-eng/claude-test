Section 02
Content Strategy & Pillars
02

Content without a system is noise. Our strategy organizes every post, reel, and story into four core pillars that work together to educate, inspire, prove results, and convert followers into paying clients.

The 4 Content Pillars
01
Educate & Build Trust
Treatment explainers, myth-busting, “Ask Dr. Uma” series, procedure walkthroughs. Positions Dr. Uma as the go-to expert.
02
Showcase Results
Before & after content, client testimonials, transformation reels. Visual proof is the #1 driver of new bookings in aesthetics.
03
Behind the Scenes
Day-in-the-life, clinic tours, Dr. Uma’s personality moments, team spotlights. Builds emotional connection and trust before the first call.
04
Convert & Promote
Special offers, seasonal campaigns, direct booking CTAs, limited availability posts. Turns followers into bookings without feeling pushy.

Weekly Posting Cadence
